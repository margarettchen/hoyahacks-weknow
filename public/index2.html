About Sexual Assault

One in five college women experience sexual assault.

Sexual violence affects hundreds of thousands of Americans each year. While we’re making progress — the number of assaults has fallen by more than half since 1993 — even today, only 6 out of every 1,000 rapists will end up in prison.

Fewer than 5% of survivors report the crime.

Every day in the U.S., over 500 women are sexually assaulted. The number doesn’t even account for all the sexual assaults that go unreported.
